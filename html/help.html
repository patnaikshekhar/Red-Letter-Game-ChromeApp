Create a Game


Create a new game when you are set to play. You may choose to practice by yourself or play with an opponent if you are all set to beat your adversary! You could elect to choose one of the below options from the menu canvas.

Have your winning name!


Before you start playing, take a moment to decide on your ‘player’ persona and give yourself your favorite name! You may also choose to set on/off sounds per your liking. In the default mode, your gaming experience is laced with sounds. Navigate to Settings in the menu canvas in order to do this.

You are all set to play!


You have 90 seconds to weave your magic and churn out as many words as you possibly can! There are letters that are associated with a higher score tag and you can get more points in your kitty by using these letters in your words. These letters are indicated in shades of red on the board. You’ll notice that as you play the letters on the board acquire different hues of red indicating to you that they are the ones you should be trying to use to score higher scores.

Warning sounds will scream out telling you that you have a countdown of 5 seconds to finish the game!

The words you construct will be displayed on the left for you to take pride in once you complete your turn!

Tips and Tricks


  • Relax, focus and enjoy your 90 seconds of play time
  • Play till you become an expert at the game of Red Letter!
  • Incorporate pricey letters in your word construction to get you a winning score
